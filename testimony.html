Testimony


Cindy :

My experience as a foreign student was a hard. From Argentina I had to gather information with Campus France, the institution with which I had to make all the stepsto be accepted in an university. My process had a preaparation aroun 1 year, I had to pass the B2 test which is the french exam required in order to study in France.
I also had to translate my high school degree, my CV, I had to write a motivation letter for each of the university I selected and I had to pass an oral interview with Campus France. Then I had to present several official papers to come in France. I waited for a comfirmattion letter freom the university and on the 4 I asked, 2 accepted me. Which one of them the UIT of Lens which was actually my first choice. After I packed all my bagages I came to France very happy, I met terreific people at UIT and I am really surprised by all the cultural differencies which you can find. It's an incredible experience that i can advice to you, beacuse knowing different culture and travelling changes your vision of the world. It also hepls to understand the way of living of the people living around the globe.
